Who We Are

Blazing Word International aka Life Abode is a ministry dedicated to raising believers on fire for God through the Word, prayer, worship and the transforming power of the Holy Spirit.

Vision

Raising a generation on fire for God through the Word and Spirit.

Mission

To disciple believers into deep intimacy with Christ and impactful kingdom living.

Our Passion

To spread the blazing fire of God’s Word, disciple lives and build a people rooted in truth, power and spiritual growth.
